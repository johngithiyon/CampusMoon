ECE Virtual Labs

Circuit Construction Kit
Interactive 3D circuit builder with realistic components. Ideal for teaching Ohm's Law, Kirchhoff's Laws, and basic circuit analysis.
Falstad Circuit
Advanced 3D circuit visualization with real-time current flow animation. Great for understanding complex circuit behavior and electronics concepts.
Ohm's Law
Interactive simulation to explore the relationship between voltage, current, and resistance. Visualize how changes in these parameters affect circuit behavior.
DC Circuit
Advanced DC circuit simulation with realistic components. Build series and parallel circuits, measure voltage and current, and explore circuit behavior.
Capacitor
Explore how a capacitor works in this interactive 3D lab. Change the size of the plates and add a dielectric to see the effect on capacitance.
Resistance in a Wire
Interactive simulation to explore how resistivity, length, and area of cross-section affect the resistance of a wire. Visualize electron flow and resistance changes.
